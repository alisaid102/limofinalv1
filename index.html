Your Premier Transportation Partner

At AllState Limo, integrity, excellence, and customer care define every ride. Serving Providence, Newport, Warwick, and Pawtucket, we’ve become Rhode Island’s trusted name in luxury limousine rental, black car service, and chauffeur-driven transportation. From executive meetings to weddings, prom limo rentals, and airport car service transfers at TF Green, Logan, and regional airports, we make every journey stylish, safe, and stress-free. Our pristine fleet is hand-selected, meticulously maintained, and includes stretch limousines, sleek sedans, and party limo rentals for any occasion. Our professional chauffeurs are extensively trained, background-checked, and dedicated to discretion and customer satisfaction. With real "limo service near me" convenience and intimate local knowledge, we ensure reliable, comfortable travel for corporate clients, wedding parties, and anyone looking for the best luxury limo experience in Rhode Island. With AllState Limo, your ride is more than transportation it’s luxury, reliability, and true peace of mind.
